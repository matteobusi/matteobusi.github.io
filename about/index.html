About me

Short Bio

I was born in Brescia, Italy in 1993.
I got my B.Sc., M.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science at University of Pisa.

My research interests include cybersecurity, programming languages, and formal methods.
Currently, my focus is on secure compilation, that is ``how can we guarantee that compilers preserve security properties?''.
